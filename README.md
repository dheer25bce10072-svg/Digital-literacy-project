Project Overview
This repository contains my Digital Literacy Portfolio created as part of the CSE0001 – Digital Literacy course at VIT Bhopal University. The project is based on the role of a Student Digital Ambassador, where the objective is to build awareness about digital literacy, create a professional online presence, explore online collaboration and coding platforms, practise email etiquette, and understand cybercrime awareness and prevention.

The repository is organized into five tasks, each corresponding to one module of the course. It also includes the final project report documenting my work, observations, and reflections.

Module / Task Summaries

Task 1 – Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic
In this task, I created a one-page infographic to explain the meaning and importance of digital literacy. The design covers important topics such as digital tools for students, safe internet practices, professional online presence, and email etiquette.

Tool Used: Canva
Folder: task-1-presentation/


Task 2 – Student Digital Portfolio
In this task, I created or updated my professional profiles on major digital platforms. This helped me understand how students can begin building a strong and professional online presence from the first year itself.

Platforms Chosen: GitHub, LinkedIn, Kaggle, 
Folder: task-2-portfolio/


Task 3 – Coding & Collaboration Platforms
This task involved exploring both coding practice and collaboration tools. I completed a beginner-level coding challenge on an online platform and created a Google Form quiz related to digital literacy awareness.

Task 4 – Professional Email & Etiquette Guide
In this task, I drafted two professional emails and created a checklist of social media do’s and don’ts for responsible online communication. This task helped me understand the importance of tone, structure, and professionalism in digital communication.

Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness Case Study & Prevention Guide
This task focuses on cybercrime awareness. I prepared a case study on a selected cybercrime topic and created a prevention checklist with practical tips for college students, including reporting channels for cybercrime in India.

Chosen Topic: Phishing
